Fire Safety Badge (Brownies)

In the second meeting of term we focused on completing the fire safety badge with the Brownies, completing this badge early on in the term was benifacial in a number of aspects as we as the leadership team we could all ensure that we were fully aware and refreshed in where the fire exits were for our meeting room, we also had many new girls join our unit so it was impotant for them to know how to safely exit in an emergency. Assisting in a fire drill is also part of the requirements for gaining your assistant leader warrent.

Badge Requirements

For the fire safety badge in brownies the following are the requirements.

  • Take part in a fire drill at your unit meeting
  • State how to call the Fire Brigade
  • Know EDITH [evacuation drill of hte home]
  • Explain why smoke detectors should be used in homes and how to test them
  • Learn how to prevent smoke entering a room and show how to safely get out of a smoke-filled room. Demonstrate how to check for fire behind a door before opening it
  • Demonstrate what to do if clothes catch on fire
  • Explain why water should never be thrown on a pan of fat which has cough fire
  • List five possible fire hazzards in a home
