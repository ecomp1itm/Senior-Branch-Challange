About Me

I am a Senior Brancher and Leader-in-Training based in the Ballincollig/Ovens District of Cork. Currently, I am a 21-year-old Computer Science student. I've chosen to build this website as my portfolio to showcase my progress while merging my passion for Guiding with my technical skills.

Each page on this site documents a specific challenge I have undertaken as part of my journey toward the Senior Branch awards.

The Challenges I've Chosen

  • 100 Points Volunteer with a Brownie Unit for a minimum of six months.
  • 50 Points Teach an Interest Badge to a Brownie Unit.
  • 50 Points Documentary-style video: The impact of social media on my generation.
  • 50 Points Attain a Bronze Star Journey Award.
  • 40 Points Visit a local Emergency Service.
  • 20 Points Start a campfire blanket/jacket.
