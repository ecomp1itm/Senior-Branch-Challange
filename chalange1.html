My Leader Warrant Journey

Challenge #1: Six Months Volunteering with Brownies

Overview

In October 2025, I began volunteering with a local Brownies unit in the Ballincollig/Ovens district. The initial phase involved completing essential requirements including Garda Vetting my purple leader forms pack and Child Safeguarding training. Below is a detailed record of my progress through various online training modules and our weekly unit meetings.

I completed my training and sent in my forms for my asstant leader warrent in December 2025, and I recived my warrant in the post on the 31/12/2025 New Years Eve.
